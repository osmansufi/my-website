Hobbies

When I'm not at school or work, or doing extra projects on the side, I have a number of other things I enjoy doing. The one thing I enjoy more than anything else is reading. But I also enjoy, almost as much, watching really great films and TV shows. I enjoyed video games in the past but I've found, with time, that I prefer spending the time I could be playing games, instead reading good books or watching good movies. Finally, I also like to keep in good shape and so I try to make sure I am regularly lifting weights and doing cardio workouts.
